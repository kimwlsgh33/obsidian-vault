# Obsidian-nvim

## Customizable checklist marks

- [ ] TODO item (- `[ ]`)
- [x] DONE item (- `[x]`)
- [>] FOLLOWUP item (- `[>]`)
- [~] CANCELED item (- `[~]`)

Concealing and highlighting of [[wiki links][https://www.naver.com]] and [external links](https://www.markdownguide.org/extended-syntax/#wiki_links)
